Welcome to

Peevees Model School

Peevees model School was established by Peevees Charitable Trust in 1999 to provide quality education to the local public with moderate and affordable fees, without donation. After 23 years of academic Excellence, Peevees Model School has now become the best CBSE school in the Malappuram district. At present, we have very good infrastructure with all facilities, such as spacious and well-equipped Science laboratories, computer labs, English language lab, Atal Tinkering Lab and Math lab to satisfy the academic needs of the students. A well-furnished KG section with an attached children's park and activity room has made Peevees unique. An excellent library with a collection of more than 80k books to enhance the knowledge and reading habits of the students. Moreover, We have clean and spacious smart classrooms, an excellent Amphitheatre to accommodate about 2000 people, a spacious conference hall, a music room with advanced musical instruments and an extensive playground. Also providing skill courses like AI, Data science, Physical education trainer and Geospatial.
